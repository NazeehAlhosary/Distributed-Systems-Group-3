System's Purpose

Group 3's system is designed to help developers furthur understand the hierarchy within their project(s). This is done by providing three different creative visualizations that illustrate the hierarchy within the inputted project(s)

Our Component Diagram

Our component diagram gives a visual describtion of how the system works on a single knowledge source.

Our Deployment Diagram

Our deployment diagram gives a visual describtion of how our nodes interact with each other using the BlackBoard Architecture Style. Our deplyment diagram provides a visual insight of the distribution of data across multiple nodes as well.
